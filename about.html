Chef Paul’s Umami Pizza Kitchen

Born in Gaborone and inspired by Botswana’s vibrant street-food culture, Chef Paul’s Umami Pizza Kitchen blends bold flavors, fresh local produce, and handcrafted dough techniques to create pizzas that taste like home with a gourmet twist.

Our Story

Founded by Chef Paul in 2024, the kitchen began as a pop-up serving neighborhoods in Gaborone. What started with a single oven and a commitment to quality quickly grew through word-of-mouth—fueled by crispy crusts, generous toppings, and the signature umami touch. Today, we’re a local favorite for events, late-night cravings, and weekend family feasts, with operations centered in Bontleng and Block 9.

What “Umami” Means to Us

Umami is the deep, savory flavor that keeps you coming back for another bite. From slow-cooked tomato sauces to roasted toppings and cheese blends, we build flavor in layers. Every pizza is crafted to deliver that rich, satisfying taste.

Values We Live By

  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients wherever possible
  • Consistency in every order—from dough to delivery
  • Community-first service and fair pricing
  • Innovation with respect for classics

What We Offer

  • Signature range: Meat Lovers, Creamy Chicken, and Mexican Chilli
  • Custom builds for private events and catering
  • Combo deals like Large + Small, On the Double, and Triple Decker
  • Flexible pickup and delivery within Gaborone (Bontleng • Block 9 and surrounding)
